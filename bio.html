Biography

My name is Pam Afaneh, I live in Arlington Texas. I have been servicing the web arena for over 20 years. I have had the pleasure of achieving a wide range of web experience, which came from my freelance roles where I worked with many different people who all had different web-related needs. For the last 5 years of my career, my development focus has primarily been on WordPress Development, as it is an easy method for small businesses that don't have a budget to keep a full-time developer on staff to maintain their site. Along with web development, I have also been heavily involved with digital marketing including Social Media Marketing, Ecommerce Development & Management, Google Adwords Campaigns, Search Engine Optimization, Branding, etc.

Even though being a jack of all trades in this field is cool & exciting. I realized that I want to take my career back to its focus. I want to be able to advance myself and become a Full Stack Web Developer. So, in summary, I just want to code. I want to leave the marketing, social and other aspects of the web behind and continue this career I adore, on the similar path it started so many years ago. With a deepened understanding, newfound abilities, and the rejuvenation of the passion I have in this field. I am looking forward to seeing what I accomplish when I complete this learning path.
